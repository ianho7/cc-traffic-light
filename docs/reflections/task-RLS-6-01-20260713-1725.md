# RLS-6-01：最终桌面影像证据例外

- Encountered Problem: Checklist initially required normal-motion screenshots or recording, but the available evidence is human observation plus state/runtime diagnostics rather than image artifacts.
- Chosen Solution: The user explicitly accepted the absence of final normal-motion screenshots/recording as a release limitation.
- Rationale: Preserve the distinction between observed behavior and saved image evidence; do not manufacture or overstate a visual artifact.
- Status: completed as an accepted limitation on 2026-07-13.
