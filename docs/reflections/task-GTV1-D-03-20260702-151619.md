- Task: GTV1-D-03 Distinguish undiscovered and untrusted states visually
- Encountered Problem: The requirements explicitly separated “not found” from “found but not trustworthy,” and collapsing them would weaken troubleshooting value.
- Thought Process: I mapped undiscovered to fully dim lamps and untrusted to uniformly gray lit lamps so the difference is structural, not just textual.
- Options Considered: Use the same dark state for both; add extra overlay text; differentiate them through lamp fill behavior.
- Chosen Solution: Differentiate them through lamp fill behavior.
- Rationale: This preserves the compact widget while still making the troubleshooting distinction visible at a glance.
